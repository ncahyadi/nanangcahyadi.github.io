Community Service Activities

SDG 4: Quality Education

Activities aimed at improving education quality include developing solution-oriented thinking at SMAN 1 Katapang, introducing Arduino workshops, and supporting the Center of Excellence Vocational School Program. These initiatives help students and educators enhance their technical and problem-solving skills.

SDG 3: Good Health and Well-being

The socialization of a photodiode-based blood type learning aid focuses on improving health education by introducing advanced tools to students, supporting a better understanding of health concepts and practices.

SDG 8: Decent Work and Economic Growth

Training programs in telecommunications and financial literacy for vocational schools aim to equip educators and students with critical skills for economic empowerment and sustainable growth in their respective fields.

SDG 10: Reduced Inequalities

Developing an interactive navigation aid for the visually impaired addresses inequality by providing tools that improve mobility and independence for those with visual impairments, supporting inclusivity and equal opportunities.
